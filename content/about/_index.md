Logimex International is a diversified international conglomerate and the parent organization for a strategic portfolio of businesses. With a legacy built on reliability and innovation, we operate at the intersection of global trade, consumer services, and asset protection.

What began as a focused logistics operation has evolved into a multi-vertical group, empowering industries and communities across the globe. Our strength lies in our ability to leverage synergies across our divisions, providing our partners with comprehensive, end-to-end solutions.

## Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value to our clients, partners, and communities through operational excellence, unwavering integrity, and a commitment to sustainable growth.

## Our Divisions

Logimex Group is organized into three key operational divisions, each a leader in its respective field:

- **Logimex Logistics** — Our founding division, providing air, sea, and land freight solutions, warehousing, and supply chain management.

- **Logimex Retail** — A rapidly growing division focused on bringing premium consumer goods and retail experiences to emerging markets.

- **Logimex Security** — Specialized security services protecting critical infrastructure, corporate assets, and personnel.

## Headquartered in Jaipur

From our headquarters in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we operate a vast network of offices, warehouses, and operational hubs. Our localized expertise, combined with our global reach, ensures that we deliver consistent, world-class service wherever our clients need us.
