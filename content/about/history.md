## Our Journey

Logimex International was founded in 2008 with a vision to revolutionize the logistics industry. What started as a small freight forwarding operation has grown into a global conglomerate spanning multiple industries.

### Key Milestones

* **2008** — Logimex Logistics founded in Jaipur, Rajasthan
* **2012** — Expanded operations to international markets
* **2015** — Launched Logimex Retail division
* **2018** — Established Logimex Security services
* **2020** — Reached operations in multiple countries
* **2024** — Celebrated {{< years from="2008" >}} years of industry leadership
