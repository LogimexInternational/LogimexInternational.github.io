Orchestrating Global Value

Beyond a conglomerate—an ecosystem of excellence.

At Logimex Group, we view business through a lens of interconnected opportunity. We are not merely a collection of companies; we are a cohesive ecosystem where strategy, infrastructure, and capital align to create market-leading outcomes.

While our subsidiaries operate independently to ensure agility in their respective sectors—ranging from complex supply chains to high-end retail—they share a unified DNA. This central "Logimex Standard" ensures that whether we are securing a critical asset or delivering premium goods, the operational rigor remains world-class.

We believe that true leadership is not about size, but about synergy. By sharing intelligence, resources, and best practices across our verticals, we insulate our partners from market volatility and provide a stability that single-vertical companies cannot match.

A Legacy of Evolution

From a single shipment to a global footprint.

Our journey is defined not by the passing of time, but by the milestones of our ambition. What began in 2008 as a focused logistics intervention in Jaipur has evolved into a multinational narrative of diversification and resilience.

2008

The Inception

Amidst a shifting global economy, Logimex International was established with a singular focus: to bring transparency and reliability to the freight sector in Rajasthan.

2012

Crossing Borders

Recognizing that commerce knows no boundaries, we expanded our operational capabilities, establishing our first international trade lanes and bridging local manufacturers with global markets.

2015

The Retail Expansion

Leveraging our mastery of the supply chain, the Group made a strategic pivot into consumer goods. Logimex Retail was born to fill a void in the market for premium, accessible lifestyle products.

2018

Securing the Future

As our physical footprint grew, so did the need for asset protection. Logimex Security was established—initially to safeguard our own infrastructure, and quickly expanding to serve external corporate clients.

2024

The Group Era

Today, we stand as a unified Group. We are no longer just service providers; we are stakeholders in the global economy, celebrating over 18 years of continuous growth.

Governance & Stewardship

Guided by experience, driven by the future.

The Logimex Group is governed by a philosophy of ethical stewardship. Our leadership team serves as the custodians of the company's reputation, prioritizing long-term stability over short-term gains.

Our Board of Directors brings together decades of expertise across international trade law, finance, security protocol, and consumer behavior. This diversity of thought allows us to navigate complex geopolitical landscapes and economic shifts with confidence.
