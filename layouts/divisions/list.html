Our Divisions

Synergy in Motion

Logimex International operates through three strategically aligned divisions—Logistics, Retail, and Security—each a leader in its domain, yet designed to function as a unified ecosystem.

Together, we manage the complete lifecycle: moving goods, bringing products to market, and safeguarding every step of the journey. This integration allows us to deliver value that no single-focus competitor can match.

Logimex Logistics

Engineering the Flow of Commerce

Precision supply chain solutions for a connected world.

Logimex Logistics is the cornerstone of the Group—the original vision from which all else has grown. From humble beginnings as a regional freight forwarder, we have evolved into a full-spectrum supply chain partner, trusted by manufacturers, trading houses, and multinational enterprises to move cargo across continents with precision and reliability.

Our approach goes beyond transportation. We engineer integrated solutions that connect every node of your supply chain—from procurement and warehousing to customs clearance, last-mile delivery, and real-time tracking. By combining deep industry expertise with cutting-edge technology, we provide end-to-end visibility and control that transforms logistics from a cost center into a competitive advantage.

Core Capabilities

Multimodal Freight

Seamless integration of air, ocean, and surface transportation tailored to cargo profiles, timelines, and budgets.

Strategic Warehousing

Climate-controlled, bonded, and open storage facilities across key trade corridors with advanced inventory management systems.

Customs & Compliance

Expert documentation, tariff classification, and regulatory liaison services to ensure seamless cross-border movement.

Logimex Retail

Curating Premium Experiences

Bridging the gap between global brands and emerging markets.

Logimex Retail was born from a clear insight: consumers in developing economies aspire to the same quality and design standards as their counterparts in mature markets. By leveraging our robust logistics infrastructure, we serve as the conduit between premium international brands and underserved consumer segments.

Our portfolio spans lifestyle categories—home décor, personal care, electronics, and wellness—with a focus on brands that share our commitment to quality, authenticity, and responsibility. We don't simply distribute products; we build brands, manage omnichannel presences, and cultivate consumer relationships that endure.

Key Focus Areas

Market Penetration

Identifying whitespace opportunities, launching brands into new markets, and establishing sustainable retail channels.

Brand Stewardship

Protecting brand integrity through authorized distribution, controlled pricing, and consistent consumer experiences.

Consumer Analytics

Leveraging data to understand purchase behavior, optimize assortments, and forecast demand with precision.

Logimex Security

Uncompromising Vigilance

Protecting critical infrastructure and corporate integrity.

Logimex Security arose organically from the demands of our own operations. As warehouses expanded and high-value cargo traversed greater distances, the imperative for professional, accountable protection became clear. What began as an internal function has since matured into a comprehensive security solutions provider—serving not only the Group but also external clients across industries.

Our personnel come from distinguished backgrounds in law enforcement, defense, and corporate security. We emphasize training, discipline, and technology-enabled operations, ensuring that every engagement reflects the standards that safeguard our own assets daily.

Operational Strengths

Critical Infrastructure Protection

Manned guarding, surveillance integration, and access control for industrial, commercial, and residential sites.

Asset Transit Security

Armed escorts, convoy protection, and GPS-tracked secure transport for high-value shipments.

Risk Consultancy

Threat assessments, vulnerability audits, and bespoke security protocols aligned with client-specific risk profiles.
