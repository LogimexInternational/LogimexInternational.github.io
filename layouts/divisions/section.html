Logimex Logistics

Moving the World, One Shipment at a Time

Logimex Logistics is our founding division—the bedrock upon which the entire Group was built. Born from a vision to transform freight forwarding in Rajasthan, we have grown into a comprehensive supply chain partner trusted by manufacturers, traders, and corporations across continents.

We don't simply move cargo; we engineer the seamless flow of goods from origin to destination. Our integrated approach combines air, sea, and land freight with warehousing, customs brokerage, and last-mile delivery to create end-to-end visibility and control for our clients.

Our Capabilities

Air Freight

Express and consolidated air cargo solutions with global carrier partnerships.

Ocean Freight

FCL, LCL, and project cargo services across major trade lanes.

Land Transport

Domestic trucking, cross-border movement, and multimodal solutions.

Warehousing

State-of-the-art storage facilities with inventory management systems.

Customs Brokerage

Expert documentation and clearance services for seamless cross-border trade.

Supply Chain

End-to-end supply chain consulting and optimization services.

Logimex Retail

Curating Premium Lifestyle Experiences

Logimex Retail was born from a simple observation: emerging markets deserve access to the same premium products enjoyed in developed economies. Leveraging our unparalleled logistics infrastructure, we bridge the gap between global brands and local consumers.

We curate, import, and distribute a carefully selected portfolio of lifestyle products—from home décor and electronics to fashion and wellness. Our deep understanding of supply chains allows us to deliver quality at competitive prices, without compromising on the customer experience.

Our Verticals

Home & Living

Premium furniture, décor, and home improvement products.

Electronics

Consumer electronics and smart home solutions.

Fashion & Accessories

Curated collections from international lifestyle brands.

Logimex Security

Protecting What Matters Most

Logimex Security emerged from necessity. As our logistics and retail operations expanded, so did the imperative to protect critical assets—warehouses, cargo, personnel, and data. What began as an internal function has evolved into a full-fledged security solutions provider.

Today, we serve external corporate clients across industries, offering a comprehensive suite of physical and operational security services. Our team comprises trained professionals with backgrounds in law enforcement, military, and corporate security.

Our Services

Manned Guarding

Trained security personnel for facilities, events, and executive protection.

Surveillance Systems

CCTV installation, monitoring, and integrated security technology.

Access Control

Biometric and card-based access management solutions.

Secure Transport

Armed escort and high-value cargo protection services.

Risk Assessment

Comprehensive security audits and vulnerability analysis.

Emergency Response

24/7 rapid response teams and crisis management protocols.
